Basic information about Amazon

Amazon was founded on July 5, 1994, by Jeff Bezos in Bellevue, Washington. The company originally started as an online marketplace for books but gradually expanded its offerings to include a wide range of product categories. This diversification led to it being referred to as "The Everything Store"

How many people use Amazon?

Amazon Has Over 310 Million Active Users Globally. With over 80% of the users from the United States, there are more than 310 million active users of Amazon globally. Around 230 million Amazon customers are from the United States.

What type of marketing is Amazon?

Amazon uses the high runner strategy to market its products. This strategy uses data to uncover which products are in the highest demand in every category. Amazon's pricing algorithm then prices those products competitively and bids heavily on advertisements to pull people to these products.
